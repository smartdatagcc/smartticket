Track Support Performance

SmartTicket will help create a transparent workflow for your business and your customers. The status, comments and uploaded documents for any ticket can be viewed at anytime. Know what has been completed and what actions need to be taken to complete each request.

Create Custom HTML Forms for Your Website

Leveraging the Custom Form Builder feature, external custom HTML forms can be built to create records within your SmartTicket system without your clients or prospective clients ever having to log in. You can create anything from patient enrollment forms to basic contact forms. With the built-in SmartTicket API, SDS can create forms matching your layout that can be placed directly on your website. When the forms are filled out and submitted on your website, the information is passed into SmartTicket and a ticket will be created automatically. Contact us for an estimate.

Read Full Testimonial

Van D Merkle DC, DABCI, DCBCN

We use the SDS internal secure and HIPAA compliant communication portal in our clinic for interoffice communications as well as communication with our patients. We often refer to this as our ‘Ticket’ system.

This communication portal or ‘ticket system’ makes the office run much smoother for the staff, doctors and patients. Our front desk staff appreciates how this portal makes it much easier to take care of patients concerns and questions. Our staff no longer has to take phone messages, relay them to the doctor, call the patient back and then having to try to make sure that all messages are documented and relayed accurately. Our patients like the ability to have access our doctors directly. All communications are filtered through our staff first and then staff will assign any necessary communications to the doctor. Once a Ticket is determined it needs to go to the doctor, then the doctor can correspond via the ticket system directly with the patient. The Ticket system has a lot of flexibility so you can customize who receives these communications.

Regular email is not HIPAA compliant and cannot be used to discuss patient matters between staff and doctors. This Ticket system allows ongoing discussion that can be sent or made available to whoever needs to be involved.

All new patients are given or sent a log in so that they can set up their account and from then on all questions or correspondence is done through it. Current patients are given the Ticket log in information as needed. (We didn’t try to get all of our patients into the Ticket system when we stated to use it.) Phone calls are now much fewer resulting in a more productive and less stressed staff.

I’m not in the office everyday but my staff and patients can still relay messages to me and I can respond in a secure manner even if I’m away on vacation or just at home.

If I had a small one doctor office, I would still use this Ticket system, because it allows me to be more efficient in dealing with patients, their problems and gives them access to me without using traditional email or the phone- basically, it will save time for the doctor and staff.

Our staff likes this Ticket system for several reasons but no longer can the patients say they didn’t get a reply and the doctors can’t say they didn’t get the message: everything is documented and time stamped.

This Ticket system can also send and receive PDF documents. Special forms, documents, diet etc. can be made available to patients via this Ticket system making the office more efficient and saving time and money from mailing out forms and documents or faxing them.

Finally, with this Ticket system we are doing everything possible to secure the patient’s confidential health records and information to the highest degree. The fact that it makes our staff more efficient is a bonus and just as important is that it gives me peace of mind that we are doing the right thing.

I highly recommend this Ticket system.

Sincerely,
Van D Merkle DC, DABCI, DCBCN
